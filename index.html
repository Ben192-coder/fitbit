Fitbit & Bellabeat Capstone

Author: Ben Halperin
Role: Aspiring Data / Business Intelligence Analyst

This project analyzes Fitbit activity and sleep data to generate insights and marketing recommendations for Bellabeat, a wellness-focused smart device company. The full report is available as an HTML document.
